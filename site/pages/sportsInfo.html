INFORMATION ABOUT SOME SPORTS

Soccer

Football or what is called (soccer) is one of the ancient and famous sports in the world, as it dates back to 2500 BC by the Chinese, but it was somewhat different from the football practiced now, which began in England in 1016 AD. This game includes matches with two teams each; Each team consists of 11 players, including the goalkeeper. Members of both teams play freely on the field and are allowed to handle the ball with all members of their body except for the hand and arm as it is forbidden to touch the ball with them, while the guards are required to protect the goal area and are allowed to hold the ball in their hands. Both sides score as many goals as possible (getting the ball into the opponent's net) to win at the end

Basketball

Basketball is one of the world-famous sports, specifically in the United States of America, where it is considered the most important games for them, along with American football, hockey, and other sports. Basketball began in 1891 in the United States of America, specifically the state of Massachusetts. The two teams, each consisting of 5 main players, compete to insert the ball into the basket. The rules of basketball: The rules of each game are the constitution for that game. There are four rounds in a basketball game each game is 20 minutes. It is not allowed for a player to take two strings with the ball without the ball touching the ground, and it is also not allowed to carry the ball with both hands

Cricket

It is an old sport that dates back to the modern era of 1744 AD in England, where it was the first official match for this game. Cricket is a team sport in which two teams of eleven players participate, in which a ball the size of a fist is hit by a player called the ball pitcher, while the opposing player is called the batsman, and he tries to block the ball using a thin paddle-like bat. Attention is mainly focused on the two marks that form the goals They call a Wicket, and they are a group of three connected sticks called "Stumps", and the pitcher tries to hit her to hit two of them fixed to the logs
